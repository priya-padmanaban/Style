Jacob and Wilhelm Grimm, were German academics, philologists, cultural researchers, lexicographers and authors who together collected and published folklore during the 19th century. The Brothers Grimm established a methodology for collecting and recording folk stories that became the basis for folklore studies. Individually, they published a large body of linguistic and literary scholarship.
